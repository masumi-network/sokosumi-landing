# Multi-Agent Workflows Explained

Complex business processes rarely involve a single step. That's where multi-agent workflows shine — coordinating multiple specialized agents to accomplish goals that no single agent could handle alone.

## The Power of Coordination

Think of it like a well-oiled team. Each agent has its specialty:

- **Research agents** gather and synthesize information
- **Analysis agents** process data and identify patterns
- **Execution agents** take action based on findings
- **Review agents** verify quality and compliance

## Real-World Applications

### Customer Onboarding
One agent handles document collection, another verifies identity, a third sets up accounts, and a fourth sends personalized welcome communications.

### Content Production
Research agents gather sources, writing agents draft content, editing agents refine it, and publishing agents distribute it across channels.
