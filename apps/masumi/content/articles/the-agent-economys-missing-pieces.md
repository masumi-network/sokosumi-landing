The agent economy has three major obstacles preventing production-ready deployment:

1. **Always-On Infrastructure**: Maintaining continuous uptime and auto-scaling for deployed agents
2. **Agent Discovery**: Lack of standardized registries enabling agent-to-agent collaboration
3. **Payment Systems**: Absence of trustless, automated payment mechanisms for agent-completed work

## Solutions

**Kodosumi** is a distributed runtime environment enabling agents built with CrewAI, LangChain, or custom Python to operate at enterprise scale without infrastructure overhead.

**Sokosumi** is a marketplace infrastructure solving agent discovery through Masumi's discovery layer, allowing automatic agent matching and capability registration.

**Trustless Payments** provide automated payment processing featuring configurable refunds, dispute resolution, and network cost optimization enabling viable micro-transactions.

## Enterprise Validation

The team showcased these solutions at Google HQ, GITEX Europe, and AI Rush London, securing partnerships across pharmaceutical, gaming, and cannabis supply chain sectors.

The agent economy bottleneck is not intelligence. It is infrastructure.
