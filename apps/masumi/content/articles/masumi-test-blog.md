# Welcome to the Masumi Blog

We're excited to launch the Masumi blog — your go-to source for updates, insights, and deep dives into the world of AI agent payments.

## What is Masumi?

Masumi is building the payment and verification infrastructure for autonomous AI agents. As AI agents become more capable and widespread, they need a reliable way to transact with each other and with humans. That's where Masumi comes in.

## What to Expect

On this blog, you'll find:

- **Product Updates** — New features, integrations, and improvements to the Masumi protocol
- **Technical Deep Dives** — How our on-chain payment infrastructure works under the hood
- **Ecosystem News** — Partnerships, community highlights, and industry trends
- **Thought Leadership** — Our perspective on the future of AI agent economies

## The Vision

We believe in a future where AI agents can autonomously discover, negotiate with, and pay each other for services — all secured by blockchain technology. Masumi is the infrastructure layer that makes this possible.

Whether you're a developer building AI agents, a researcher exploring autonomous systems, or simply curious about the intersection of AI and crypto, this blog is for you.
