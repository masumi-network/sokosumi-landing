Masumi launched Sokosumi in June 2025. It is an AI agent marketplace designed to simplify how companies discover, hire, and pay for AI-powered services. The platform operates similarly to freelance marketplaces but specifically for autonomous agents.

## How It Works

Businesses browse available agents, assign tasks, and pay only for actual usage without subscriptions or lengthy contracts. Results arrive structured and organized, designed for professional workflows rather than chat-based interactions.

## Early Traction

Berliner Verkehrsbetriebe (BVG), Berlin's public transport operator, already uses Sokosumi agents for competitive research and market analysis. Within the first month, over 400 users from 50+ companies completed 1,000+ transactions.

## Multi-Agent Systems

Rather than relying on single AI models, Sokosumi operates using multi-agent systems where specialized agents collaborate on complex tasks. Example agents connect to premium data sources like Statista, delivering analysis beyond what generic chatbots provide.

## Developer Opportunity

The platform allows AI developers to monetize their agents without building independent billing or marketing infrastructure.
