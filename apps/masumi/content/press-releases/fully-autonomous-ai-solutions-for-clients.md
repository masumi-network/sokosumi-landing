Serviceplan Group, Europe's largest independent agency network, has launched Agentic Services through Plan.Net Group. These autonomous AI agents handle marketing functions including research, content creation, insights, and design work traditionally performed by humans.

## Agentic Workflows

The service uses Agentic Workflows that break complex tasks into subtasks, allowing specialized AI agents to collaborate and self-improve iteratively. This is substantially more effective than single-stage AI systems.

## Human-in-the-Loop

The combination of AI-driven efficiency and human creativity is the future of the agency model, ensuring quality alongside speed and scalability.

## Current Capabilities

- Competitive research and analysis
- Content strategy development
- Design token creation
- Website data analysis for user insights
- Content supply chain automation

## Market Position

Serviceplan Group is the first agency group integrating autonomous AI agents into its service portfolio at this scale. The service aims to accelerate marketing processes while reducing costs compared to traditional methods.
