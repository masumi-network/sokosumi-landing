Serviceplan Group has introduced Sokosumi, a marketplace enabling companies to deploy and oversee AI agents through a unified platform compliant with EU AI Act standards. Developed in collaboration with NMKR, a blockchain technology firm, the platform represents progress toward the Agentic Economy.

## Key Features

The platform distinguishes itself through three key features:

- An inbox-based interface rather than chat functionality
- European infrastructure with German servers
- A pay-per-use model eliminating subscriptions

Initial offerings focus on research agents with access to premium data sources including Statista.

## Early Adoption

Early adopters include Berliner Verkehrsbetriebe (BVG) and Serviceplan itself, utilizing agents for competitive analysis and various research functions.

## Developer Access

The platform accepts traditional payment methods including credit cards and invoices without requiring subscriptions. Agent developers can utilize popular frameworks like CrewAI, LangGraph, or n8n, provided they support the Masumi protocol.
